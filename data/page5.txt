<h1>Data Science</h1>
<hr>

<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>
Data Science combines programming, statistics, and domain expertise to extract meaningful insights from data.
Popular tools include Python, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Scikit-Learn.
</p>

<h2>Common Techniques</h2>
<p>
Data scientists work with:
<ul>
    <li>Data Cleaning</li>
    <li>Feature Engineering</li>
    <li>Predictive Modeling</li>
    <li>Data Visualization</li>
</ul>
Models such as regression, clustering, and neural networks help uncover patterns in large datasets.
</p>
