<h1>Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning</h1>
<hr>

<h2>What is AI?</h2>
<p>
Artificial Intelligence (<strong>AI</strong>) focuses on building systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. 
These tasks include reasoning, perception, language understanding, and decision-making.
AI systems are transforming industries such as healthcare, finance, transportation, and customer service.
</p>

<h2>What is Machine Learning?</h2>
<p>
Machine Learning (<em>ML</em>) is a subset of AI that uses statistical techniques to enable models to learn patterns from data.
Common ML techniques include:
<ul>
    <li>Supervised Learning</li>
    <li>Unsupervised Learning</li>
    <li>Reinforcement Learning</li>
</ul>
Popular algorithms include neural networks, support vector machines, and decision trees.
</p>
